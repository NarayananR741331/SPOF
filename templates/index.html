How It Works

.drawio Diagrams

Parses XML graph structure, finds articulation points (critical SPOFs), hub nodes, and single-connection components.

.docx Documents

Extracts text and scans for 15+ SPOF patterns: single databases, missing redundancy, monolithic architecture, and more.

Images & Visio

For best results, export to .drawio format. Images are accepted but require the source file for full graph analysis.

All analysis runs locally on your machine. No data is sent to any external server.
